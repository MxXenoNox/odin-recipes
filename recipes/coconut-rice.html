Coconut Rice

Description

An easy and quick side dish whenever you're in a tropical mood or homemade Asian takeout!

Simply add all to a rice cooker and hit go!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups long-grain jasmine rice
  • 1 ~ 3 clove(s) garlic, minced
  • 3/4 t. kosher salt
  • 1 13.5 oz. can coconut milk
  • 1 c. water

Directions

  1. Gently rinse the jasmine rice in cold rice until it runs clear. Place the rice in a bowl and fill with water then leave it to soak for 30 min. Add the rice (including the starchy water), minced garlic, and salt to the slow cooker.
  2. Add the coconut milk. Give the rice a brief stir--gently so you don't break the rice!)
  3. Start the rice cooker and cook on low for 15 min.
  4. Let the rice rest covered for additional 5 min. Then remove the lid and fluff the rice with a fork. Enjoy!
